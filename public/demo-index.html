Thanks for visiting the demo version!

  • CREATE a goal by entering the name of the goal (start and end dates are optional) and click on the 'Create goal' button
  • Select a goal under the 'Goals' section
  • Enter an action for selected goal and click 'Submit action'
  • Each action submitted will trigger an animation
  • To DELETE a goal select goal and click on the X button
